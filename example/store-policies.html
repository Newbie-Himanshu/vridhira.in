Shipping Policy

history Last updated: October 24, 2023

We want you to be completely satisfied with your unique handmade purchase. Because every item on ArtisanMarket is crafted by an independent artist, shipping times and methods may vary slightly, but we hold all our sellers to the following high standards.

On this page

  • arrow_right_alt Processing Time
  • arrow_right_alt Estimated Delivery
  • arrow_right_alt Tracking Your Order
  • arrow_right_alt Customs & Import Taxes
1

Processing Time

The time I need to prepare an order for shipping varies. For details, see individual items. Generally, since items are handmade with care:

  • In-stock items: Ships within 1-3 business days.
  • Made-to-order items: Can take 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity of the craft.
  • Custom commissions: Timelines will be agreed upon directly with the artisan via messages.
2

Estimated Delivery Times

Once your order is dispatched, estimated transit times are as follows. We do our best to meet these shipping estimates, but cannot guarantee them.

Region Standard Shipping Express Shipping
North America 3-5 business days 1-2 business days
Europe 7-14 business days 3-5 business days
Asia Pacific 10-20 business days 5-7 business days
Australia & NZ 12-25 business days 5-10 business days
3

Tracking Your Order

All orders include a tracking number which will be emailed to you once the label is created. You can also view tracking information by visiting My Orders in your account dashboard.

info

Please note that tracking updates may take 24-48 hours to appear in the carrier's system after you receive the shipment notification.

4

Customs and Import Taxes

Buyers are responsible for any customs and import taxes that may apply. ArtisanMarket is not responsible for delays due to customs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my shipping address after ordering? expand_more
If you need to change your shipping address, please contact the seller immediately. If the item has not yet shipped, they can usually update the label. Once an item is in transit, we cannot redirect it.
Do you offer gift wrapping? expand_more
Many of our artisans offer gift wrapping services for a small additional fee. Check the individual item listing or message the seller directly to inquire about gift options.
What if my package is lost? expand_more
If your package hasn't arrived within the estimated window, please check your tracking number. If it appears lost, contact the seller first. If you cannot resolve the issue, ArtisanMarket Support can step in to assist.
